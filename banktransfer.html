Bank Transfer to the OpenBSD Foundation

Donating to the Foundation by bank transfer is very possible. The procedure may vary according to where in the world you are sending funds from. The information below is provided to us by our bank, which is the Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce (CIBC). It is hoped that you can show this page to your bank to make this process easy for you.
